About BEETA

Charting a new orbit for auctions

BEETA Auction House brings together rare artistry, rigorous authentication and a global bidding network — built on trust that reaches as far as it aims.

Our Company

Precision-run auctions, globally trusted

BEETA Auction House is an independent auction house specializing in fine art, jewelry, antiques, automobiles, real estate and rare space memorabilia. Operating across Pan-India and South India regions with growing international reach, we serve private collectors, institutions, estates and consignors who expect precision at every step.

Our team combines specialist appraisers, legal advisors and dedicated regional coordinators to ensure every lot — from a signed mission patch to a museum-grade sculpture — is authenticated, catalogued and sold with complete transparency.

🎯

Our Mission

To connect collectors and consignors worldwide through auctions defined by authenticity, transparency and uncompromising quality — from earthbound heirlooms to artifacts touched by space itself.

🔭

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted name in specialty auctions across India and beyond — where every hammer fall reflects integrity, expertise and genuine value.
